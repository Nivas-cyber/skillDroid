What we do

Practical skills,
not slide decks.

Most cybersecurity courses are just video lectures. At SkillDroid.in, every topic comes with a lab environment where you practice on real tools — Burp Suite, Metasploit, Nmap, Wireshark, Volatility — the same stack you'd use on the job.

Our instructors are active security professionals. Not YouTubers, not content creators — people who do this work daily and teach from experience.

03
Curriculum aligned to industry certs
Our content maps to CEH, OSCP, eJPT, and CompTIA Security+ so your learning transfers directly to certification prep.
04
Private community and job support
Discord access, resume reviews, mock interviews, and referrals to security teams actively hiring.
